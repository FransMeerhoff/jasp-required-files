diet {flexplot}R Documentation

Diet dataset

Description

This dataset seeks to examine how effective certain diets can be for losing weight.

Usage

diet

Format

A data frame with xx rows and 6 variables:

Person

description

gender

The subject's gender

Age

Age of the subject

Height

Height of the subject

pre.weight

Weight of the subject before the diet

weight6weeks

Weight of the subject after 6 weeks

Diet

Type of diet the subject went on

graduate_income {flexplot}R Documentation

Graduate income

Description

This dataset examines whether schools and academic performance can lead to higher income in professions. Simulated data

Usage

graduate_income

Format

A data frame with xx rows and 4 variables:

Grad.School

Whether the subject attended grad school

Profession

Profession of the subject

GPA

GPA of the subject

Income

The current income of the subject

Years

Years since they graduated

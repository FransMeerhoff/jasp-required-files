plant_growth {flexplot}R Documentation

Plant Growth

Description

Measures of seedling growth depending on various characteristics. NOT simulated data.

Usage

plant_growth

Format

A data frame with xx rows and 5 variables:

Location

Location stored (greenhouse vs inside)

Soil.Type

Type of soil used

Method

Method of planting (soil blocks versus newspaper)

Diameter

Diameter of trunk

Leaves

Number of leaves

alcuse {flexplot}R Documentation

Alcohol use among youth

Description

A dataset containing alcohol use from 82 youth over the course of three years

Usage

alcuse

Format

A data frame with 246 rows and 9 variables:

ID

ID of participant

AGE

AGE of participant

COA

Are they a child of an alcoholic? 1=yes

MALE

Gender (1=male)

AGE_14

Number of years since age 14

ALCUSE

Average number of alcoholic drinks a week

PEER

Average number of alcoholic drinks a week among their peers

CPEER

Median-centered amount their peers drink

CCOA

Child of alcoholic, centered

NSDUH Dataset

Description

This is the 2014 National Survey of Drug Use and Health (NSDUH) dataset

Usage

nsduh

A data frame with xx rows and 27 variables:

used alcohol recently?

used cocain recently?

her.rec

alc.freq

frequency of alcohol use

cocain.freq

frequency of cocain use

heroin.freq

frequency of heroin use

cig.freq

frequency of cigarette use

inpatient

have they received inpatient treatment?

outpatient

have they received outpatient treatment?

moves.past5

moved in the past 5 years?

attend.rel.serv

do you attend religious services?

rel.inf.dec

how influential is religion on your decisions?

distress

mental distress score

whodas.impairment

mental impairment score

MI

probability of mental illness

major.dep

ever had major depression

dep.pas.yr

depression in the past year

age

age

military

been in military

health.rating

self reported health rating

sex

sex

education

educational attainment

race

race

county

type of county they live in (small metro, large metro, etc)

income

how much bank they make

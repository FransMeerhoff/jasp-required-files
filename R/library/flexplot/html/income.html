income {flexplot}R Documentation

Simulated Dataset About People's Income

Description

A dataset containing 207 observations, containing the following information

Usage

income

Format

A data frame with 207 rows and 6 variables:

stats_skills

performance on a statistics know-how test

group

Treatment group (control, mo money, wealth building)

graduate

hs graduate vs. college graduate

hours

hours they work a week

income_before

Income before starting training

income_after

Income after starting training

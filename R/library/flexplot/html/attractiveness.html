attractiveness {flexplot}R Documentation

Simulated Dataset About People's Attractiveness

Description

A dataset containing 207 observations, containing the following information

Usage

attractiveness

Format

A data frame with 207 rows and 5 variables:

stats_skills

performance on a statistics know-how test

group

Treatment group (control, watching hitch, receiving stats training)

gender

Gender of person being rated

exercise

Self-reported average number of minutes exercised per week

attractiveness_1

Attractiveness score of person being rated at baseline

attractiveness_2

Attractiveness score of person being rated at follow up

age

Age of respondent

gender

Gender of respondent

political

Political Ideology of respondent
